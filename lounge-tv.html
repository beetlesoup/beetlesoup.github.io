Single Drunk Female (2022)

Hey, Sophia Black D'Elia! Her character here is a perfect continuation of the one she played in The Mick, even though it's a totally different person. I can't speak for virgin viewers people who haven't seen it, but a LOT of the appeal for me came from this: it is not a spinoff, but it feels like one.

Where Sabrina was a rich kid with liberal views, a general apathy about her life, and a fear of going to college, Samantha is a 28 year old alcoholic who has to attempt a fresh start because she pretty much used to be... Sabrina.

Even the tone of the new show (light drama, muted comedy) shifted from the old one (wild sitcom) in a way that feels, to me, like Sabrina herself took the control away from Mick. <3
