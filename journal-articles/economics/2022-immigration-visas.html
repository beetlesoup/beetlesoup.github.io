The Effects of High-Skilled Immigration Policy on Firms: Evidence from Visa Lotteries (Link)
KIRK DORAN, ALEXANDER GELBER, ADAM ISEN on Journal of Political Economy08 September 2022

We compare winning and losing firms in lotteries for H-1B visas, matching administrative data on these lotteries to administrative tax data on US firms and to approved US patents.

[USA] Firms that hire H-1Bs grow faster and innovate more because they are different in other ways from firms that do not. Additional H-1Bs have insignificant and at most modest effects on firm innovation.
