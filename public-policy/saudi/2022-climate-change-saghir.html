Climate Change in Saudi Arabia: Rethinking the Role of Local Government (Link)
BILAL SAGHIR on King Salman Center for Local Governance18 May 2022

National-level policy is critical in promoting and supporting the work of local governments to mitigate and adapt to climate change. In addition to adequate institutional capacity and support, central and regional government can help promote local efforts by:

Recommendations:

  • Municipal competition with financial rewards to encourage local efforts.
  • Bi-directional communication to ensure that local actions are aligned to national priorities, and lessons learned and successful models created by localities are translated to the national level and disseminated where they can support other regional and local efforts.
