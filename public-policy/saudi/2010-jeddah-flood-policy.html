Changing Public Policy Due to Saudi City of Jeddah Flood Disaster (Link)
NAILL M MOMANI, AYMAN S FADIL on Journal of Social Sciences30 September 2010

Recommendations:

  • Pursue a new approach in the process of notification and warning of rain and floods
  • Activate the role of maintenance of equipment in the rainy seasons
  • Preparing hydrological studies to address road issues
  • Activate the role of the community in awareness and prevention
  • Review regulations related to disaster management and urban planning, particularly with regard to identifying the urban boundaries of each city
  • Establishment of anti-corruption institutions
