How

What you need is to get on laptop (not phone) and open Goodreads. You will repeat this process many times, over many days:

0. If you haven't already, you'll need to add the most salient books to your library, by giving them a star rating or adding them to one of the main shelves. Personally, I've added 2 custom bookshelves to the main shelf group (to-read, reading, and read): did-not-finish and will-not-read.

1. Look up a book you had strong feelings about (positive, negative, or mixed)

2. Look through the comments for people who articulate your feelings about this book WELL. If you hate the writing, look for reviews that describe what it is about the writing that they hated. etc. Pick one of these reviews.
